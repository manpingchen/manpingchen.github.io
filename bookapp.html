Book a Meet APP

  • UI
  • UX

As some people have so many books hoarding at home without reread, how to reuse the pile of books at our home valuably?

Book a Meet App is aimed to provide connections between people who have similiar interests in reading. A book in the system is more than a book, it could be a common interest between two people.

'Lend' and 'Read' are the core actions in the whole system, which is aimed to give a learning and sharing opportunity for ourselves.
