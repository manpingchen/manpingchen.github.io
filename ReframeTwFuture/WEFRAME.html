Who are we

We are a community of designers, engineers, planners and art managers. We are also bloggers, gamers, bakers and hikers.

Our philosophy is that we all have multiple roles that shape who we are and how we see our world. Thus we create our culture of the company as an on-line learning community as we believe social learning should be diverse and up-to-date. It is a key to bring our multi-dimensional life experiences, rather than just work experiences, into our company for empathizing, defining the right challenge and creating the human-centred design.

We aim to reframe our society to be more inclusive. With the understanding of the complexity, we work with multiple-disciplinary professionals, public sectors, startups and organisations, keep the human-centred design thinking at the centre of our work and value what we bring into real world.

How we work

We use human-centred design methodologies as the core of the whole process with theoretical and cross-professional backgrounds as an in-depth dialogical research. For keeping us focus, being inspired and ideate, we develop our method kits. We experimentally implement our ideas into the real life and see how it works carefully to ensure what we do has positive impacts on society.
