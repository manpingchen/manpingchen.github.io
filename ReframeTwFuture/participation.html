Participatate In Building A Talking Wall

Build a talking wall to create a place that encourages more discussions and bottom-up participations of the issues related to the future of Taiwan.

How to participate in

I have a wall!

To build a wall that is free to talk about the issues, we need walls that are from who wants to encourage social participation. So if you have a wall and want to take part in the building event, please drop a mail to us with the following information:

  • The size of the wall
  • The address of the wall
  • The issues you want to encourage more discussions
OK! Mail!

I want to help!

To build a wall that is free to talk about the issues, we need people to help us to clean, paint, write or draw the wall. You don’t need to be an artist, a painter or a designer. All you need is to bring an open mind. We love to see people who try new things with an experimental attitude. Normally, it will be a one-day event. If you want to take part in, feel free to mail us with the following information:

  • Your name
  • The counties that you can participate in.
  • The issues you want to encourage more discussions
OK! Mail!

Want to talk about another topics?

Here are some topics we suggest

  • Meaningful work based on the current working culture in Taiwan
  • The diversity of art and design facilitated in society based on the current aesthetic education in Taiwan
  • The encouragement of interaction and communication in the society based on the current social atmosphere
  • Reframing the social expectation based on the social pressure we have been experienced

Dos and Don'ts

Please mind the following information to create a better society that is value-led living.

Dos

Be open-minded when participating in the co-building event.

Feel free to share your perspectives on the wall, no matter if you are the cobuilder or just walk by and want to share.

Respect other people whose perspectives are different from yours.

Don'ts

This is for encouraging the social participation and exploring the diversity of perspectives, any aggressive or insulting word is not allowed. It those words are founded on the wall, they will be removed.

This is for the public to participate in discussing social issues, do not use as a tool for commercial or any promotion of a particular product.
