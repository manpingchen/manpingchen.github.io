Summary

10/10 Psychological Clinic is a psychological clinic in Taiwan. With its relaxing-designed and the slick layout, it has earned a lot of positive feedback from a range of end users.

To provide an inclusive and approachable user experience, the design process included case studies based on the existing services in Taiwan, colour decisions with provided branding guideline and open-narrative icon design.

It is featured with an online booking form with email notifications and an admin portal for information management.
