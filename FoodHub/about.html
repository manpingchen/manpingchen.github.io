Research Book

This is a three-month research project which is aimed to redirect the urban future.
From the aspects of well-being living in the urban city and environments that form people's behaviours, first, I had studied the materialism, consumerism, zero-waste lifestyle and minimalism to understand the relationship between material and mental satisfaction. Second, under the life being affected by Global Captialism culture, the duplicated environment to create convenient spaces for consumers has been changing the behaviours and mindsets. Thus, having the understanding about the environmental facts and formed mindsets and behaviours, I found grocery shopping could be the startpoint where consumers and sellers can participate in the change for the future urban living.
