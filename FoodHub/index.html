Local food in the future

The project is aimed to reframe the urban food consumption. A circular system is designed to optimise the sustainability of daily food consumption. The core object of the system is the fruits and vegetables. Each fruit or vegetable is beyond itself, which is about nutrition, climate, time and materials. Research Book

Grocery

While we live in the consumerist lifestyle, the over consumption and over disposal are the burden of our planet. The way of displaying food uses the ripe level to show the time and show the knowhow about how to eat them in different level. It encourages people to buy the food mindfully and learn about the different periods of fruits and vegetables naturally.

Source

The fruit and vegetable source of the food shop could come from community garden, city farms nearby and local people's fruit trees since now more and more people and groups grows edible plants. Providing a place to buy the local growth can encourage people consume seasonal fruits and vegetables which is also more healthy for our land.

Waste

The unsold fruits and vegetables will be destructed by the next purposes, such as deseeded to be planted, compost and material making.

Material

According to Wrap Future Food report, there is a total of 7 million tons of household food waste. However, fruits and vegetables waste have many purposes that we can easily reuse in a open-loop way. For instance, the citrus peels is an ingredient of all-purpose cleaner, banana peels can be made as fibers and pineapple leaves can be leather.
