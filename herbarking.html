Her Barking

  • Social Design
  • Graphic Design

Good News!We’re delighted to share that we’ll be taking part in London Festival of Architecture this summer with HerBarking.

Her Barking is a project aimed to recreate the space safer and friendly for women in Barking. In this project, we used collaborative design in workshops.

After identifying the most unsafe places, the second workshop was aimed to invite local people to collaboratively design those places. By doing so, people can also change the perceptions of making a real difference. The question for them to ideate was 'If you were holding your best friend's birthday party in this place, what would you do?'.

In this workshop, the participants were given permission to use their creativities to shape the places.

After the workshop, people decided the perfect birthday party for each location. And then, we cooperated with a spatial designer to sketch them out!
