Summary

Chapter 2 Selection is a second-hand online bookstore focusing on mental health issues. With a designed challenge-based filter, users are able to find the right books based on the challenges they are going through.

It is featured with different topic-based filters, form validation, lending system, checkout, and an admin portal for assets management.
